About Our Company

Your Trusted Business Partner

FAKO & BROTHERS HOLDINGS PTY LTD is a diversified company incorporated in Lesotho, offering a comprehensive range of services across multiple industries. Our company is fully compliant with all Lesotho tax regulations and holds valid certifications for all our business activities.

Registered on 07 April 2025 under the Companies Act 2011, we have quickly established ourselves as a reliable business entity with a commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction.

Our main business address is located at Motsekuoa Ha Bele Holima Motsekuoa High School, Mafeteng, where we coordinate our diverse operations across the country.
